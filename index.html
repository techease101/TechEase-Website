About TechEase

TechEase is a locally owned, senior-focused tech support service founded by Victor Duarte. We prioritize patience, clarity, and real, hands-on solutions — not scripts or sales.

Our mission is to bridge the digital divide by making technology accessible and less intimidating for seniors and community members of all technical skill levels.

  • Free initial consultation
  • On-site & remote help
  • Group workshops & staff training
  • Flexible scheduling
  • No long-term contracts
